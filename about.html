About Task Manager

Task Manager is a simple and efficient web application designed to help you organize and manage your daily activities. You can add tasks, set deadlines, track your progress, and keep everything in one place.

This platform is perfect for students, professionals, or anyone who wants to stay productive. With a user-friendly interface and powerful features, you can easily create, update, and delete tasks. Your data is securely stored using MongoDB in the backend.

Whether you're managing personal goals or team projects, Task Manager makes it easy to stay on track and get things done. We are continuously working on adding new features to improve your experience.
